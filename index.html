What to weigh before rating
  • Team energy & morale — engaged, or running on fumes?
  • Sustainable pace — late nights, weekend work, or burnout signs?
  • Confidence in the current plan — does the team believe the work is achievable?
  • Psychological safety — are blockers and disagreement raised openly?
  • Team friction — interpersonal or cross-team tension affecting delivery?
